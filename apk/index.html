Features

  • ⚔️ Advanced bot that simulates and picks the best possible move to beat you.
  • 🃏 Build your hand from packs — 6 guaranteed Level 1 cards + 1 bonus card with rare drop chances.
  • 🤝 Bot tries to match your hand power for fair games.
  • 🧠 Same and Plus rules included. Capture with style.
  • 💸 Sell cards to the game. But each sale reduces the card’s price by half forever.
  • 🃏 Each starter pack bought increases the next price by 1%.
